Case Study: Using Both Po.st Tools To Drive Powerful Results

While each component of the Po.st platform is significant, combining both the URL Shortener with the Sharing Tool provides a very powerful instrument to capture and take advantage of these audience insights. Ultimately there is a wealth of sharing and engagement data that these two tools collect and can be utilized by brands and publishers. We decided to take a look at one of our customers that takes advantage of both Po.st tools and showcase the added benefits of an integrated sharing and social strategy.

A world renowned culinary magazine utilizes both the Po.st Sharing Tool as well as the Po.st URL Shortener, capturing sharing activity and generating cohesive audience insights for their entire online audience. With one platform, all of their user data is collected in one spot, from those interacting with content directly through their website to those users engaging with their brand through social media. This data gathered through Po.st provides valuable insights that enables this brand to expand their overall audience pool by retargeting current and new users through paid media.

The technology and features behind the Po.st Sharing Tool and URL Shortener capture these audience insights in a number of different ways. We’ve outlined several of these methods that they implement and described them below:

Shortened Links & Branded Vanity Domains

Shortened links from the Po.st URL Shortener that are embedded directly in their shared posts, Tweets, and other social media, drive engaged users directly to the the culinary brand’s company website. This brand in particular uses a vanity domain to keep their social media links and posts short and sweet, as well as provide the added benefit of an extra bit of branding in each link. In this way they captures new users who discover their content through social media and drives them to interact with their brand further on their website.

Social Sharing Buttons

Once a user is interacting with content directly on the brand’s website, the Po.st Sharing Tool provides engaged users with a multitude of options to share this content with their social networks. Whether you Pin It, Tweet it, or even share it through Reddit, Po.st provides each user a way to connect with their friends on social media. From clicking on the brand’s shortened link, to arriving on the culinary brand’s web page, to sharing a new article they may have found on their website, Po.st can aggregate all of this engagement across channels.

Branded Vanity Domains Integrated With Social Sharing Buttons

The company also utilizes their branded vanity domain outside of their own social media links created by the Po.st URL Shortener. Through the Po.st Sharing Tool, Po.st offers the ability for users who are sharing their content through Twitter or LinkedIn to show vanity domains shortened links directly in their shares.

 

 

 

 

After-the-Share Ad Units

The Po.st Sharing Tool has the ability to show After-the-Share units that monetize the most engaged users who enjoy the content and want to share it with their friends. For this brand since each share unit includes a custom vanity domain short link, those users who click that shared link can also be targeted with the brand’s paid media from URL data as well.

  

Copy & Paste Sharing

The Po.st Sharing Tool also provides a unique feature that captures users who share content by copying-and-pasting sections of an article or page. This widget pops up when readers highlight a section of text, prompting them to share that information. This helps boost the number of shares of the culinary brand’s content, increasing the number of users receiving and reading their articles.

When you combine the Po.st Sharing Tool with the Po.st URL Shortener you begin to truly capture your entire audience, giving you the most comprehensive insights from all sharing and social engagement activity. Whether they are users who are spending a lot of time on your site, or people just discovering your content through social, the power of Po.st gives you the opportunity to get to know your newest as well as most loyal consumers.

Overall this culinary magazine brand utilizes both the Po.st Sharing Tool as well as the Po.st URL Shortener in tandem to drive user engagement with their content. With a significant increase in engagement since they began using Po.st last fall, it is safe to say that they are savoring the sharing!
